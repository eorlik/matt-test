I create music and sound for theatre, film and TV, in bands and as a solo artist. I have created work with the BBC, the Royal Opera House, Jeremy Deller, The Barbican, The Guardian, Novara Media and more, with projects receiving awards from Tribeca Film Festival and The Grierson Trust.

I am one half of the band Strange Boy and release solo music on my own label Circle Dance, as well as on Bedroom Community, Cantaloupe Records and Glass Guts. I also produce left wing political education projects with The World Transformed.

Combining classical training with an interest in contemporary music, my work spans styles and settings but is often socially or politically engaged and linked by a collaborative and conceptual approach.

I grew up in Bridport, Dorset, and now live in London.
